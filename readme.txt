So it is a shooters game. Use mouse to control camera, left click to shoot.

You need to shoot the agents to increase score, shoot as many as you can in 30 seconds.

There are 30 agents at the begining of the game. Agents will be destroy on hit. For every 30 seconds, the manager will reset. The score will reset and agents number will back to 30.

Agents will not move too far from the player. Every time the player hit an agent, every other agents in a certain distance will be pushed away.
